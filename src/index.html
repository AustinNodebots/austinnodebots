Austin NodeBots Day

July 27th, 2014

NodeBots Day is back for 2014. We'll join together across the world to create and learn.

NodeBots Day is not a hackathon. There will be no prizes other than the satisfaction of making something awesome! There will be very little in the way of talks. We're going to get together, collaborate and hack. The focus of NodeBots Day is on sharing and enjoying each other's company. And JavaScript. Lots of JavaScript.

We are working on a lot of things right now for the event:

  • Sponsorship - So you don't have to pay as much.
  • Ticketing - There's a limit to how many people we can buy kits for.
  • Scheduling - just to sort of give some order to the day's events
  • Logistics - Getting the venue in order, stocked, and supplied
  • Device Starter Kits - Waiting to hear back from some sponsorship opportunities
  • Project Kits - Give a little focus to the day with a cool project to build.
  • The Great Unknown - so many things just pop up

We will also have Donovan Buck as a speaker. He will introduce you to NodeBots, Johnny-Five, and hopefully some demos of what is possible with NodeBots.
