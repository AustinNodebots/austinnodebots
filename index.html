Austin NodeBots Day

July 27th, 2014

NodeBots Day is back for 2014. We'll join together across the world to create and learn.

NodeBots Day is not a hackathon. There will be no prizes other than the satisfaction of making something awesome! There will be very little in the way of talks. We're going to get together, collaborate and hack. The focus of NodeBots Day is on sharing and enjoying each other's company. And JavaScript. Lots of JavaScript.

Tickets

Get your tickets to this years event. There are two options this year:

  • Attendee
  • Attendee + Project Kit

Project Kits - the project is going to include wood cut frame for a SoccerBot (Sumobot with a mod to the front), 3d printed the front ball bearing holder, a couple of continuous servos, battery holder, batteries, and various supplies to connect it all. Speaker

We have the privilege of having Donovan Buck introduce you to NodeBots, Johnny-Five, and a demo of what is possible.

Here's a little bit about Donovan:

Donovan is an aspiring roboticist and a contributor to Johnny-Five, the Open Source, JavaScript Arduino programming framework.

Donovan is a strong believer in the value of continuing education in Computer Science. His own CS education began at 5, ferrying punch cards around the data center for his dad and swapping out magnetic tapes while perched atop boxes of printer paper. At 13, he landed his first paid job building and repairing CP/M-based personal computers.

In the mid-90s, Donovan co-founded Bravo Zulu Interactive and later merged in 2006 with BrandExtract. He is now VP of Software Engineering at BrandExtract.
